Rotterdam — Studio Suiker

The best listing on the whole page, and the only one you can un-book. 4.98 from 143 reviews, top 5% on Airbnb, Superhost couple, a real enclosed tiled bathroom (sliding door + glass-paned inner door), roof terrace, two bikes included — and the week extension drops the nightly rate. The one question: it sits in Overschie’s old village core, ~10 min by bus or bike from Rotterdam Centraal — village-walkable, not city-center-walkable. That trade also keeps you clear of Zomercarnaval’s parade weekend downtown.

Franeker · Friesland

Ashton’s add · cheapest Non-refundable Eisinga Planetarium 2 min walk · ~2h20 by train from Apeldoorn

The “Moto” apartment in Franeker’s historic core — an Elfstedentocht city with the world’s oldest working planetarium around the corner. Bathroom photo-verified: enclosed room with a real lockable door, marble walk-in shower behind glass, toilet inside. The review Ashton linked praises the upstairs fan for sleeping. Trade-offs: kitchen has fridge + microwave but no stove; the double bed is in an A-frame attic up open-tread stairs; two reviews mention hearing neighbors; 12 reviews on the listing and the business host averages 4.52 across 242; and the rate is non-refundable — a commit-now choice. It’s also the deepest into the map: lovely for Friesland, long for the 1 Aug hop to Berlin.

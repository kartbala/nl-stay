The pick

The Hague

Widest choice of well-reviewed sub-€150 places, and the best base for the car-light-urbanism rides — Delft’s woonerf and Rotterdam are both a short train away. Leiden is the calm runner-up if you’d rather a smaller canal town closer to Amsterdam.

Avoided — Rotterdam & Gouda

Rotterdam Zomercarnaval (~1M visitors; street parade Sat 25 Jul) lands mid-stay — it’s in Rotterdam and overflows into Gouda 20 min away. Both sell out and spike on the 24–25th. Off the list.

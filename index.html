01 What's off the table for these dates

Two cities have a timing problem in our exact window. The rest are clear.

Excluded
Nijmegen

The Vierdaagse marches (21-24 July) and the Vierdaagsefeesten festival (18-24 July) land right on our stay -- roughly a million visitors. Rooms are sold out or priced at multiples. Great city, wrong week.

Caution
Amsterdam as a base

The Weeknd and Playboi Carti play the Arena on our check-in weekend (17-18 July), which spikes rates, and the city tax is about 12.5%. Fine to day-trip in for the med-locker pickup, weaker as a home for a week.

Clear for the window: Haarlem, Utrecht, Delft, Leiden, Rotterdam, Den Bosch.

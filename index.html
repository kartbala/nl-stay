Köln Belgisches Viertel — with the City Flat held as backup

Franeker · Friesland

Most car-free feel Non-refundable Eisinga Planetarium 2 min walk · ~2h20 by train from Deventer

The “Moto” apartment in Franeker’s historic core — an Elfstedentocht city with the world’s oldest working planetarium around the corner. On the location rule it scores high in miniature: the whole canal-ringed core is five minutes across on foot and effectively car-light — but it is a town of ~13,000, so “close to the city center” means a small-town center, not a city one. Bathroom photo-verified: enclosed room with a real lockable door, marble walk-in shower behind glass, toilet inside. The review Ashton linked praises the upstairs fan for sleeping. Trade-offs: kitchen has fridge + microwave but no stove; the double bed is in an A-frame attic up open-tread stairs; two reviews mention hearing neighbors; 12 reviews on the listing and the business host averages 4.52 across 242; and the rate is non-refundable — a commit-now choice. It’s also the deepest into the map: lovely for Friesland, long for the 1 Aug hop to Berlin.
